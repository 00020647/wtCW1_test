Samarkand State School #35

Samarkand State School #35 is a prestigious public state school in Samarkand. With its history and recognition, the School maintains its dedication to excellence and up-to-date education.

School History

Samarkand State School #35, established in the well-known lawyer’s mansion, has a rich history of providing quality education. The Samarkand State School #35 has been operating even during WW2. The School was also operating as female-only school shortly after WW2 for 2 years. Over the years, the school has become a cornerstone of academic excellence, nurturing generations of students in a dynamic and supportive environment.
